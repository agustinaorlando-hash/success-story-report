Most clients don't share publicly due to audience mismatch — their external channels aren't HR-focused. Vélez, for example, shared the case internally with employees but not on their public accounts, which they reserve for fan content.
🗺 Action plan — starting now
Once a client approves their case, we send a ready-made email to encourage sharing on their own channels. Depending on the case, we offer one of these:
  • ~30-second short-form version optimized for social
  • Branded visual asset (plaquita) with key stats
  • Ready-to-use copy for client to post with minimal effort
Día Argentina was the first case shared through this initiative. This process is now established — amplification will be tracked systematically from Jul 2026 onwards.
